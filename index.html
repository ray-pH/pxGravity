Available variables:
n_particle (number) : number of particle (N particle in Simul Option)
px (Float32Array) : x position of the particles
py (Float32Array) : y position of the particles
vx (Float32Array) : x velocity of the particles
vy (Float32Array) : y velocity of the particles
m (Float32Array) : mass of the particles
Math (object) : "library" of mathematical functions (javascript)
